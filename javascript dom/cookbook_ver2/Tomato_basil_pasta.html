Tomato and basil pasta

by random guy

Ingriedients

  • Dry pasta
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Parmezan cheese
  • Basil

Method

  • Put pasta on boil
  • fry chopped galic in olive oil in a frying pan
  • add tomatoes

  • When pasta ready drain and mix with sauce garnish with basil
